Who's this Kid?

The name's Aditya Prakash. I am a kid born and raised in Giridih, Jharkhand.

I am a student of Aerospace Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, and expect to be graduated by June 2023. The one thing I love about myself is my curiosity to learn new things. Since my childhood, I wanted to become a scientist and an astronaut. However, with age, the adventurous (in physical world) part of me faded away and only the curiosity remained.

My interests lie in Space Dynamics and Rocket Propulsion with an inclination towards theoretical physics. Other than this, I am interested in Cognitive Psychology, Analysing NeuroImaging Data and Deep Learning Models. And I love Web Development

Previously I have worked on Facial Emotion Recognition under BCS IITK. To make free educational content on the internet more accessible, I developed: Project Ekatra, which I am improving to make more user-friendly.

To know more about my research interests, visit my research page.
