Who's this Kid?

A curious nerd with rocket boots, controlling the space.

The name's Aditya Prakash. I am a kid born and raised in Giridih, Jharkhand.

I am a student of Aerospace Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.

My interests lie in Space Robotics, Control Theory, and Spacecraft Autonomy. Building robots that dance among the stars, because who needs gravity when you have thrusters? If it isn't space, then it's entropy that keep me hooked. Other than this, I am interested in Cognitive Psychology (where does intuition come from?), Analysing NeuroImaging Data (to find the answer to the question before) and Deep Learning Models (the magic box which has become quite magical in recent days). And I love Web Development.

To know more about my research interests, visit my research page.
